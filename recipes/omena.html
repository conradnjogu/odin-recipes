An estimate of two million Kenyans depends on the Omena processing chain to earn a living. Ingredients

  • 2 cups of omena
  • 3 cups of water
  • 1/2 of cooking oil
  • 2 large onions
  • 1 cup of milk
  • A teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. First step is to wash the omena in cold water
  2. Place the omena in a pan and let it simmer gently in 3 cups of water until the water is almost evaporated
  3. Dip the tomatoes in boiling water and then take them out for peeling
  4. Chop the tomatoes
  5. Heat the oil in the frying pan
  6. Stir in the onions and tomatoes and cook until soft
  7. Stir gently to avoid crushing the omena
  8. Add the milk and let it cook for 30 minutes. Milk will act as gravy
  9. Once ready serve with hot ugali and a vegetable of your choice
